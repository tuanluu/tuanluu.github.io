About me

Hi! I am Luu Anh Tuan, an Assistant Professor at the College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Before that, I was a postdoctoral fellow at MIT CSAIL and a member of the Natural Language Processing group under the supervision of Prof. Regina Barzilay from 2018 to 2020. I obtained Ph.D. degree in computer science from Nanyang Technological University in 2017.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing with the focus on applications of Large Language Models, Robustness & Trustworthy AI, Question Answering, Information Extraction, and Graph Neural Networks.

Email: anhtuan.luu@ntu.edu.sg
Office: N4-02c-86, College of Computing and Data Science, NTU

Note: I am currently interested in taking new PhD students. Please feel free to contact me if you are very interested in deep learning and NLP. If you are one of the top students from a good university, there are many excellent PhD scholarships such as the AISG PhD Fellowship, Nanyang President's Graduate Scholarship, and IPP Scholarship.

Note: I also have some positions for research fellows, research associates, and research assistants. If you have a strong publication record in NLP and AI and would like to do cutting-edge research in NLP, please feel free to contact me with your CV.
